All postings to info-octave are also sent to the help-octave mailing list, so you don't need to subscribe to both info-octave and help-octave.

Messages sent to the info-octave mailing list are not archived separately, but they do appear in the help-octave list archive.

Joining the Mailing Lists

If you would like to join the discussion and receive all messages sent to these mailing lists, please send a message containing just the word subscribe in the body to help-subscribe@octave.org (to join the help list), or bug-subscribe@octave.org (to join the bug list), or sources-subscribe@octave.org (to join the sources list), or graphics-subscribe@octave.org (to join the graphics list), or maintainers-subscribe@octave.org (to join the maintainers list), or info-subscribe@octave.org (to join the info list).

Similarly, to unsubscribe, send a message containing the word unsubscribe in the body to one of the above -subscribe addresses. If you can no longer send mail from the account you originally used for subscribing, send a message with

unsubscribe old-address@your.domain

in the body.

Please do NOT send subscription requests directly to the lists themselves.
