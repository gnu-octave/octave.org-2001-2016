Octave 2006
Washington, DC - April 20-21, 2006

The first Octave Workshop will be held April 20-12, 2006 at the National Institute of Standards and Technology in Gaithersburg, MD USA.

This will be a gathering of Octave developers and users to provide a forum for discussing the current state and the future of Octave and its application areas. Topics include language improvements, numerical methods, application areas, proposed extensions and infrastructure developments.

Gaithersburg's location near Washington DC, Annapolis and Baltimore provides easy access to tourism and entertainment opportunities for conference attendees and their accompanying persons.

Check the Call for Papers for submission guidelines and important dates.

We have no specific hotel arrangements. The NIST web site has generic visitor information for NIST and Gaithersburg.

A tentative schedule of talks is available. See the list of participants for abstracts and links to papers. Octave 2006 Schedule
